Introduction

Campus recruitment is the process by which the corporate recruit students who are about to graduate from the educational institutions. The selection process varies from company to company, may have pre placement talk, written test, group discussion and interviews.

Objective

Our objective is to help the students to get to know about factors in which company hire students and give them higher salary. Campus recruitment is one of the major reasons which help students to get a better career. This will greatly help colleges to boost their placement percentage and to train student accordingly.

Students will be well prepared for placement they will get to know what packages they can expect from the company based on their previous education and work experience. The website will be updated every year so that we can get new data regarding placement and our model can be more precise. This project can be used by the placement cell in every college.
